SIMAZI

SIMAZI is a comprehensive education management platform designed to support various roles within an academic institution—administrators, institution responsibles, teachers, and students. The application facilitates efficient management of institutions, academic content, and user interactions through its modular structure. Here's a brief overview:

The system centralizes and automates processes related to:

Institution & User Management

Administrators have dedicated routes to add, modify, and delete institutions. They also manage different types of users such as admins, profs, responsables, and students, ensuring the right roles and responsibilities are assigned across the platform.

Academic Content Management

The platform allows for comprehensive CRUD operations on levels (grades) and subjects (matieres), including the management of domains linked to each subject. Teachers can add and edit lessons, set objectives, manage exercises, and update questions, ensuring the curriculum is dynamic and interactive.

Group & Class Management

Institution responsibles can create and manage groups or classes. They can assign levels, connect teacher-matière associations, and manage student enrollment within these groups, thereby facilitating an organized classroom setup.

Interactive Learning Tools

For teachers and students, the application offers interactive features such as:

  • Lesson and exercise management for creating a robust learning environment.
  • Online quizzes and question banks to assess student progress.
  • Real-time tracking of student performance and responses.

User Experience Enhancements:

The system includes customizable user settings, such as the ability to toggle between dark mode and light mode, providing an adaptable and user-friendly interface.
