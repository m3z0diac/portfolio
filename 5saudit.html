5SAudit

The 5S Audit Application is a web-based tool designed to help companies manage and improve workplace organization through regular audits and planning. It supports the 5S methodology (Sort, Set in order, Shine, Standardize, Sustain), which is used to maintain efficiency, cleanliness, and safety in work environments—especially in industrial or manufacturing settings.

Here's what the app allows users to do:

Conduct Audits

Users can start, run, and stop audits on various work areas. These audits are used to assess how well the 5S practices are being followed.

Manage Auditors (Auditeurs)

Companies can add, edit, or remove people responsible for performing these audits.

Manage Equipment (Engins)

The app keeps track of machinery or tools that are part of the audit scope.

Manage Rooms or Zones (Chambres)

Users can organize areas within a facility that will be regularly audited.

Plan Audits (Planning)

The app allows users to schedule future audits, so that assessments are done regularly and efficiently.

Analyze Results

Users can view reports and analysis of past audits to monitor progress and areas needing improvement.
