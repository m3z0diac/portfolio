MYOEA

This application is designed to streamline and manage several key processes related to training sessions, participant attendance, payments, invoicing, student progress, and certifications. It serves administrators, instructors, and students by offering a centralized platform for managing documents, payments, attendance, and progress tracking.

Core Features

    Invoicing and Payment Management:

    • Admins can create and manage invoices for students, download them, and track payments.
    • Options for both regular and manual invoices and payment follow-ups are available.

    Certification Management:

    • Participants can receive certifications after completing training or courses.
    • Admins can download and manage these certificates for students.

    Progress Tracking:

    • Admins can track the academic or training progress of students.
    • Progress sheets can be generated and downloaded, including grades and attendance for each session.

    Attendance Monitoring:

    • The system allows administrators to track student attendance for each session.
    • All attendance data is organized and downloadable.

    Student and Grade Management:

    • Admins can manage student information and grades.
    • Features to download grade reports for individual students and groups are available.

    Financial Management:

    • Income and outcome data related to the educational institution are tracked.
    • Downloadable reports are available for both income and outcome categories.

    Supply Management:

    • Supplies related to courses or sessions can be managed.
    • Related documentation can be downloaded.

    Insurance and Outcome Management:

    • The application handles insurance documents and outcome reports.
    • These can be downloaded for further analysis or record-keeping.
